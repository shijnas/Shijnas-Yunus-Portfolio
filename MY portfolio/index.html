TESTING WORKFLOW

My Bug Hunting Protocol

01

Play & Explore

Deeply explore gameplay mechanics, map boundaries, menus, and new updates to understand expected behavior.

02

Identify Issues

Spot deviations, crashes, performance drops, collision issues, or logic gaps that disrupt the player flow.

03

Reproduce Consistently

Isolate variables to document a reliable 100% reproduction path. Identify the exact triggers of the glitch.

04

Document Clearly

Write highly professional, easy-to-follow bug reports with specs, steps, expected/actual results, and media proof.

05

Verify & Close

Once developers release a fix, re-test aggressively to ensure the bug is resolved and no new regressions arose.

BUG-104 Priority: High Severity: Medium

Character gets stuck while entering a vehicle

Build: v1.0.4-live
Platform: Windows PC
Category: Animation / Physics

Steps to Reproduce

  1. Spawn a standard 4-door sedan.
  2. Stand directly next to the driver's side door.
  3. Press the enter key F repeatedly while moving forward.
  4. Observe player model freezing mid-animation.
Expected Result

The character smoothly initiates the entry animation and transitions to the driver's seat.

Actual Result

The player model freezes halfway through the animation. Controls lock out. Player remains stuck until disconnecting.
